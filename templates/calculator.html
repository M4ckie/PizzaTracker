Dough Making Instructions

  1. Cool water to <60°F.
  2. Mix the water and active dry yeast.
  3. Add flour and Olive Oil and mix for 2 minutes.
  4. Keep mixing on low, then add Sugar and Salt.
  5. Mix for 10 more minutes.
  6. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and rest for 1–3 hours.
  7. Divide the dough into portions.
  8. Shape into balls, sealing the seam.
  9. Put the dough in a lightly oiled container.
  10. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 2–4 days. 3 days is ideal.
  11. Let the dough come to room temperature before using.

Gluten-Free Dough Making Instructions

  1. Add cold water and yeast to bowl.
  2. Add gluten-free flour (I recommend Caputo) and Olive Oil and Mix for 2 minutes.
  3. Keep Mixing on low, and then add sugar and salt.
  4. Mix for 3–4 minutes until completely combined.
  5. Cover the bowl and put in fridge for 15 minutes (helps firm up the dough).
  6. Remove bowl and oil up your hands.
  7. Shape dough into dough balls (doesn't need to be perfect).
  8. Put the dough balls into a lightly oiled container.
  9. Cover tightly and refrigerate for up to 48 hours.
  10. If using same day, leave out on counter 3–4 hours before baking.
  11. Let the dough come to room temperature (2–3 hours) before using (covered).
